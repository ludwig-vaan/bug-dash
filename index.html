Bug Dash !

How to play ?

Your goal is to reach the water, without colliding into any one of the enemies (bugs).

You can move ←, →, ↑, and ↓ with the keyboard. The enemies move in varying speeds on the paved block portion of the scene and speed increase each time you reaches the water !

Once you collides with an enemy, you moves back to the start square and lose a heart point!

If you lose 5 heart, it's game over !

Each time you reaches the water, you win a point
